1. POMONA BLEND
================================================================================

PRIMARY USE: Orchards (Stone Fruits, Pome Fruits, Pistachios)

DESCRIPTION:
Pomona Blend is a targeted micronutrient application optimized for tree crops, 
specifically designed for stone fruits (peaches, plums, cherries, apricots, 
nectarines), pome fruits (apples, pears), and pistachios. This blend provides 
essential nutrients during critical growth phases to support fruit development, 
nut quality, and yield consistency.

KEY CHARACTERISTICS:
• Granular formulation
• OMRI Listed for organic use
• Made in USA
• Optimized for tree crop nutrient requirements

APPLICATION:
• Timing: Post leaf-out (typically April–May)
• Method: Applied within tree drip lines for maximum root zone efficiency
• Purpose: Provides micronutrient support during active photosynthesis period
• Benefits: Directly impacts nut development, fruit quality, and yield consistency

TARGET CROPS:
• Pistachios
• Stone fruits (peach, plum, cherry, apricot, nectarine)
• Pome fruits (apple, pear)
• Other high-value orchard crops

WHY POMONA FOR ORCHARDS:
Stone fruits and orchard crops have similar nutritional profiles and growth 
patterns. Pomona Blend is the closest match to their specific needs, providing 
targeted micronutrient support that enhances fruit set, color, consistency, 
and overall tree health.

SPEC SHEET REFERENCE:
See "Pomona blend specsheet.docx" and "pomona blend specsheet png.png" in 
Supporting Documents folder for complete technical specifications.

2. SERIKAI'S SECRET BLEND (SERIKAIS BLEND)
================================================================================

PRIMARY USE: Avocados and Citrus Trees

DESCRIPTION:
Seriokai's Secret Blend (also referred to as Serikais Blend) is specially 
formulated for avocado and citrus trees. This granular, OMRI-listed blend 
combines premium organic ingredients to boost root strength, fruit yield, 
and disease resistance while improving soil structure and water retention.

KEY CHARACTERISTICS:
• Granular formulation
• OMRI Listed for organic use
• Made in USA
• Specially formulated for avocado and citrus trees

INGREDIENTS & PURPOSE:

1. WORM CASTINGS
   Purpose: Enhance soil structure, aeration, and water retention; provide 
   balanced, slow-release nutrients (N, P, K); introduce beneficial 
   microorganisms for improved soil health and disease suppression.
   
   Benefits:
   • Improves soil structure and porosity
   • Increases water-holding capacity
   • Provides slow-release nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium
   • Introduces beneficial soil microorganisms
   • Enhances natural disease suppression

2. ZINC SULFATE
   Purpose: Supplies essential zinc to correct deficiencies, supporting enzyme 
   activation, protein synthesis, and robust root development.
   
   Benefits:
   • Corrects zinc deficiencies common in avocado and citrus
   • Supports critical enzyme functions
   • Enhances protein synthesis
   • Promotes strong root development
   • Improves overall tree vigor

3. POTASSIUM SULFATE
   Purpose: Delivers potassium and sulfur to boost water retention, 
   photosynthesis, protein synthesis, and plant resilience.
   
   Benefits:
   • Improves water retention in plant tissues
   • Enhances photosynthesis efficiency
   • Supports protein synthesis
   • Increases plant resilience to stress
   • Improves fruit quality and size

4. ORGANIC DAIRY COMPOST
   Purpose: Improves soil structure, nutrient content, and microbial activity 
   for better water retention and sustainable plant growth.
   
   Benefits:
   • Builds organic matter in soil
   • Improves soil structure and tilth
   • Enhances microbial activity
   • Increases nutrient availability
   • Supports long-term soil health

APPLICATION:

FOR NEW PLANTINGS:
• Mix 1 part Blend with 4 parts existing soil
• Ensures proper nutrient availability from establishment
• Creates optimal root zone environment for young trees

FOR ESTABLISHED TREES:
• Top-dress with a 1/2" layer around tree base
• Gently work into the soil surface
• Water thoroughly after application
• Apply within drip line for maximum effectiveness

KEY BENEFITS:
• Specially formulated for avocado and citrus trees to boost root strength, 
  fruit yield, and disease resistance
• Enriches soil with essential nutrients and beneficial microbes for 
  sustained, balanced growth
• Improves soil structure, water retention, and aeration for healthier, 
  more resilient trees
• Supports organic gardening with premium, natural ingredients for long-term 
  soil vitality

TARGET CROPS:
• Avocados
• Citrus (oranges, lemons, limes, grapefruit)
• Other subtropical tree crops

WHY SERIKAI'S BLEND FOR AVOCADOS AND CITRUS:
Avocados and citrus have unique nutritional requirements, particularly for 
zinc and potassium. They also benefit significantly from improved soil 
structure and water retention. Seriokai's Secret Blend addresses these 
specific needs with targeted ingredients that support root health, fruit 
development, and overall tree resilience.

PACKAGING SIZES:
• 9 LB bags
• 1 CF bags
• 2.2 CY TOTE
• BULK (TONS/CY)
• TRUCKLOAD BULK (22-24 TONS)

3. BUCCHAS BLEND (VINES)
================================================================================

PRIMARY USE: Vineyards (Wine Grapes, Table Grapes, Raisin Grapes)

DESCRIPTION:
Bucchas Blend is specifically formulated for vineyard applications, designed 
to support grape vine health, fruit quality, and yield stability through 
improved nutrient uptake and soil biology. This blend addresses the unique 
needs of grape production systems.

KEY CHARACTERISTICS:
• Formulated for vine crops
• Optimized for grape production systems
• Supports quality and yield stability

APPLICATION:
• Timing: To be determined based on vine phenology
• Method: Applied within vine rows or under vine canopies
• Purpose: Supports nutrient uptake and soil biology for grape production

TARGET CROPS:
• Wine grapes
• Table grapes
• Raisin grapes
• Other vine crops

WHY BUCCHAS BLEND FOR VINEYARDS:
Vineyards have distinct nutritional and soil health requirements compared to 
tree crops. Grape vines benefit from specific nutrient profiles and soil 
conditions that support quality fruit development, sugar accumulation (brix), 
and overall vine health. Bucchas Blend is formulated to address these 
vineyard-specific needs.

4. BLEND SELECTION GUIDE
================================================================================

Use this guide to determine which blend is appropriate for each crop type:

ORCHARDS (Stone Fruits, Pome Fruits, Pistachios):
→ Use POMONA BLEND
• Best match for stone fruit nutritional profiles
• Optimized for tree crop growth patterns
• Supports fruit development and quality

VINEYARDS (Grapes):
→ Use BUCCHAS BLEND (VINES)
• Formulated specifically for vine crops
• Addresses grape production requirements
• Supports quality and yield stability

AVOCADOS AND CITRUS:
→ Use SERIKAI'S SECRET BLEND (SERIKAIS BLEND)
• Specifically designed for avocado and citrus trees
• Addresses zinc and potassium needs
• Improves soil structure and water retention

6. INTEGRATION WITH SSW PROTOCOLS
================================================================================

All blends work in conjunction with SSW's comprehensive regenerative soil 
health protocols:

SUPPORTING PROTOCOLS:
• Orchard/Vineyard Compost Blend: High-quality dairy compost for organic 
  matter building
• Compost Tea Extract (Drench): Living microbial inoculant for biological 
  activation
• Clean Wood Fiber: Mulch application for erosion control and moisture 
  retention
• Mycorrhizal Backfill Mix: Specialized fungal blend for new plantings

PROGRAM APPROACH:
Each blend is part of a complete regenerative program, not a standalone 
product. The blend application is coordinated with:
• Soil testing and analysis
• Customized protocol development
• Seasonal application schedules
• Ongoing field support and monitoring
