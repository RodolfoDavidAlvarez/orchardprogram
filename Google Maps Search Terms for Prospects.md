## üí° PRO TIPS FOR GOOGLE MAPS SEARCHING

1. **Use Satellite View**: Switch to satellite view to visually identify orchards/vineyards
2. **Zoom In**: Look for patterns of trees/rows that indicate commercial operations
3. **Check Business Listings**: Click on locations to see business names, websites, and contact info
4. **Save Locations**: Use Google Maps "Saved" feature to create lists by crop type
5. **Use Street View**: Verify operations are active and commercial-scale
6. **Cross-Reference**: Combine with county agricultural records and USDA data
7. **Search Nearby**: Use "Search nearby" feature after finding one orchard to find others
8. **Filter by Reviews**: Operations with reviews are often established businesses
9. **Check Hours**: Operations with listed hours are commercial businesses
10. **Look for Signs**: Street view often shows farm names on signs/gates
